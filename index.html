Market Thesis: The 2026 Transition

As of Q2 2026, the artificial intelligence infrastructure market has reached a critical bottleneck. Conventional electrical interconnects are failing to meet the bandwidth-density requirements of 1.6T and 3.2T networking. This has triggered a mandatory industry pivot toward Silicon Photonics (SiPh), Co-Packaged Optics (CPO), and Heterogeneous XPU Architectures.

This registry represents a category-defining collection of namespaces for the next-generation compute stack. Whether defining the Fabric of an OCS network or the Inference logic of a sovereign AI cluster, these assets provide the technical authority required for market leadership.
